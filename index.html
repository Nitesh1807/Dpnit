Terms & Conditions

Welcome to DpNit! By using DpNit to shop, sell, or just browse, you agree to these Terms and Conditions. Please read them carefully.

1. Your Account

You must provide accurate information (Full Name, Email, Phone Number, and Addresses) when creating an account. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure.

2. Buying on DpNit

  • Payments: We accept secure online payments (via Razorpay) and Cash on Delivery (COD).
  • Shipping: Delivery times vary based on the seller's location. We partner with third-party logistics (like Shiprocket) to deliver your drops.
  • Returns & Refunds: You can request a refund or return through your "My Orders" dashboard if an item is defective, damaged, or not as described. All return requests are subject to Admin review and approval.

3. Selling on DpNit

  • Listing Items: All products submitted by sellers go through an Admin Approval process before going live. We reserve the right to reject listings that violate our guidelines.
  • Accuracy: You must provide accurate descriptions, prices, and stock quantities. You are required to upload at least one real photo of the item.
  • Health & Food Items: Any listing in the "Health & Food" category must include a valid FSSAI License Number.
  • Fulfillment: You must provide a valid Pickup Address. When an order is placed, you are responsible for packing the item securely for our logistics partners to pick up.

4. Prohibited Items

You may not sell: counterfeits, replicas, illegal items, weapons, stolen goods, or anything that violates local laws.

5. Account Termination

We reserve the right to suspend or ban accounts that engage in fraud, sell fake items, or repeatedly violate our Community Guidelines.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile Data: Name, email, and profile photo (via Google Auth or manual signup).
  • Contact & Location: Shipping addresses (for buyers), Pickup addresses (for sellers), and phone numbers.
  • Transaction Data: Order history, wishlist items, and payment status (we do not store your credit card numbers; these are handled securely by Razorpay).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill orders and arrange shipping pickups/deliveries.
  • To communicate with you about your account, order updates, and support requests.
  • To maintain a safe marketplace by reviewing reported products and resolving return disputes.

3. Sharing Your Information

We only share your data when absolutely necessary to run the marketplace:

  • Logistics Partners: We share your address and phone number with shipping partners (like Shiprocket) to deliver your orders.
  • Payment Gateways: We share transaction details with secure payment processors (like Razorpay).
  • Sellers: Sellers only receive the information necessary to fulfill your specific order.
  • We will never sell your personal data to third-party marketers.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting our support team.

Community Guidelines

DpNit is built on trust, hype, and authenticity. To keep the platform safe and high-quality for everyone, all users must follow these rules:

1. Keep it Authentic (No Fakes)

DpNit has a zero-tolerance policy for counterfeit goods, replicas, or unauthorized copies. If you didn't buy it legit, don't list it here. Sellers caught dropping fakes will be permanently banned.

2. WYSIWYG (What You See Is What You Get)

Your product photos must be real pictures of the actual item you are selling. No stock images from Google. Your description must honestly state the condition of the item — if a sneaker has a scuff, mention it.

3. Respect the Community

Whether you are leaving a product review or reporting an item, keep it respectful. Hate speech, harassment, bullying, or discriminatory language will result in immediate account deletion.

4. No Off-Platform Transactions

To protect buyers from scams and ensure sellers get paid, all transactions must happen through the DpNit checkout system. Trying to bypass the platform by sharing UPI IDs or direct phone numbers in product descriptions is prohibited.

5. See Something, Say Something

If you spot a fake item, a scam, or an inappropriate listing, use the "Report this product" button on the product page. Our Admin team reviews every single report to keep the feed clean.
